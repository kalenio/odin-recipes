Overnight Oats

Image: Several jars of overnight oats with various toppings including fruit, granola, and nuts.

Overnight oats are the perfect meal if you're lazy, cheap, and enjoy food that is both healthy and tasty. They're packed full of nutrients and highly customizable, and this variant is made with protein powder for a whopping 54g of protein per serving.

Details

Prep time: 15 minutes

"Cook" time: Minimum 12 hours in fridge

Makes: 1 serving

Materials

  • 1/4 cup whole oats (quick oats won't work, as the result is too mushy)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp hemp hearts
  • 1 tbsp shredded coconut
  • 1/4 cup protein powder (any flavored powder like vanilla or mocha will work)
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk (I use soy, but cow, almond, or coconut works, too)

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl, then transfer to a 16oz wide-mouth mason jar or bowl or large cup or whatever you have on hand.
  2. Allow the mixture to sit in the fridge for at least 12 hours, then serve. Consume within 3-4 days.

Nutritional Info (approximate)

  • Per serving: 610 kcal, 22g fat, 45g carbs, 14g fiber, 54g protein
